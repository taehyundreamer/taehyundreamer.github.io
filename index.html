Research

Industry-Region Fit Framework

My primary research introduces the Industry-Region Fit (IRF) Framework, which explains how the alignment between a startup's industry characteristics and its regional ecosystem features drives venture success. Using a dataset of 1,600 startups across four major U.S. innovation hubs (Silicon Valley, Boston, New York, Austin), this work provides empirical evidence that regional context is not merely a backdrop but an active determinant of startup outcomes.

Research Interests
